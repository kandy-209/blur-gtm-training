# Sales Training AI Enhancements

This directory contains integrations and enhancements for the sales training AI system.

## Integrated APIs & Services

### 1. Company & Contact Enrichment
- **Clearbit Enrichment API**: Company data, employee count, revenue, industry
- **ZoomInfo API**: Contact information, job titles, company insights
- **Apollo.io**: B2B contact and company database

### 2. Email & Communication
- **Hunter.io**: Email verification, finding, and domain search
- **SendGrid**: Email template testing and delivery analytics
- **Resend**: Modern email API for transactional emails

### 3. AI & Analysis
- **OpenAI/Claude**: Enhanced prompt engineering for better role-play
- **Google Cloud Natural Language**: Sentiment analysis
- **AWS Transcribe**: Speech-to-text for call analysis
- **AssemblyAI**: Real-time transcription and sentiment

### 4. Prospect Research
- **LinkedIn Sales Navigator API**: Professional profiles and insights
- **Crunchbase API**: Company funding, news, and growth metrics
- **SimilarWeb**: Website traffic and competitive intelligence

### 5. CRM Integration
- **Salesforce API**: Sync practice scenarios with CRM
- **HubSpot API**: Contact and deal management
- **Pipedrive API**: Pipeline tracking

## Features

### Enhanced Persona Generation
- Auto-enrich personas with real company data
- Add realistic company context (revenue, size, industry)
- Generate more accurate objections based on company stage

### Email Template Generator
- AI-powered email template creation
- Personalization suggestions based on prospect data
- A/B testing for subject lines and CTAs

### Prospect Research Tool
- One-click company research
- Contact discovery and verification
- Competitive intelligence gathering

### Call Analysis
- Transcribe practice calls
- Sentiment analysis of responses
- Identify improvement areas automatically

### Pipeline Tracking
- Track practice scenarios as deals
- Measure conversion rates
- Identify best-performing techniques
